Fiddle Leaf Fig

The Fiddle Leaf Fig, scientifically known as Ficus lyrata, is a popular indoor plant known for its large, glossy leaves that add a touch of tropical beauty to any space...

Care Tips

Light

Prefers bright, indirect light. Avoid direct sunlight to prevent leaf burns.

Water

Water once the top inch of soil is dry. Avoid overwatering as it leads to root rot.

Humidity

Fiddle Leaf Figs enjoy high humidity. Consider using a humidifier or misting occasionally.

Soil

Use well-draining potting soil with peat to retain moisture without waterlogging.
