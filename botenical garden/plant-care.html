2. Sunlight Requirements

3. Soil and Fertilization

Soil quality is essential for plant health. Learn about the best types of soil and how to fertilize your plants to ensure they grow strong and healthy.

4. Pruning and Cleaning

Pruning helps maintain the shape of your plants, encourages new growth, and removes dead or damaged parts. Find out how to prune your plants properly.

5. Repotting Your Plants

Repotting gives your plants room to grow and refreshes their soil. Learn the best time to repot and how to do it properly.
