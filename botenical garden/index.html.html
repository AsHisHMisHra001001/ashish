Lodhi Garden, located in the heart of New Delhi, is a botanical paradise known for its beautiful landscapes, diverse plant life, and vibrant seasonal flowers. Spread across 90 acres, the garden showcases a wide variety of trees, flowering plants, and lush green lawns, making it an ideal spot for plant lovers and nature enthusiasts.
